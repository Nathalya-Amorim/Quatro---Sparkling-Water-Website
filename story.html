Our Story

Quatro fruit is a healthy, refreshing and natural drink.
The recipe is simple: sparkling water + real squeezed fruit.
No sodium. No sugar. No calories.

Discover our story

Our farmers,
our fruit.

Quatro was a fruit-flavoured canned carbonated drink and was
first produced in 1982 in the UK.
Its name was derived from the four fruits used; lemon,
lime, orange and grapefruit.

Last year, the company was redesigned. Now with a more
modern visual, with a new citrus flavour with the fruits: lemon,
lime, orange, grapefruit. Much more delicious and refreshing.

It is currently sold in most stores, from the local supermarket to
major stores like Walmart and the Real Canadian Superstore.
